All about Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan, born February 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York, USA, is an American professional basketball player who plays as a shooting guard and nicknamed Air Jordan.

Early experience

Childhood Michael Jordan was born on February 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York, United States. When he was five years old, his family moved to North Carolina. When Jordan was a child, Jordan had a good relationship with his father. Jordan's famous tongue-spitting action when he dunked came from his father's action in repair work. At that time, the two I'm very enthusiastic about baseball. When Jordan was a child, he was very naughty. In high school Jordan's basketball career started at Wilmington Laney High School, but the young Jordan was not eye-catching. The following year, he was only 5 feet 11 inches tall, and he was thin and small. He was relegated from the first team to the second team by his coach. However, Jordan did not give up. By the time of his senior year of high school, he was selected into the All-American high school student lineup.

In college In college College (18 photos) On March 29, 1982, as a freshman at the University of North Carolina, Jordan scored the winning goal in the final of the NCAA League, helping North Carolina win 63-62 against Georgetown University, led by Ewing. In the 1983-84 season, Jordan was once again named College Player of the Year and First-American Player by Sports News.

Career
